Welcome to Border Gardening

Established in 2003, we are a family run business, based in Dukinfield, Cheshire.

We provide a tailor-made, first class service to all our customers.

We have many happy existing customers and we are more than happy to provide references from them. As we are a small company, all work is done on a personal level, so there's no communication break down between the customer requesting a job and it getting done, as there can be with larger firms.

Our Services

March to October

These months are primarily Garden Maintenance, including lawns, borders and hedges. We cover both the private and business sectors, tailoring the service to suit the customer.

November to March

In these months, we carry out winter garden tidy-ups, patio jet-washing and paved areas, as well as gutter cleaning and repair, small brickwork, pointing, tiling & joinery, painting & decorating, plus a host of odd jobs, so its best to contact us to see if we do it!
